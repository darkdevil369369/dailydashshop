Refund Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Digital downloads

Because our products are instant digital downloads, all sales are generally final. That said, your happiness matters to us — so we always try to make things right.

When we'll refund or replace

  • The file is faulty, corrupted, or won't open.
  • You received the wrong item.
  • The product is materially not as described.

In these cases, contact us within 14 days of purchase and we'll send a fixed file or issue a refund.

What isn't covered

We can't refund for change of mind, buyer's remorse, lack of the required software to edit a file, or accidental purchases where the file was already downloaded — but reach out and we'll do our best to help.

How to request

Email hello@dailydashshop.com with your order number and a short description. We reply within one business day.
